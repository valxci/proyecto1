About us

This is a space created by and for Pokémon fans. Here, you'll find news, character analyses, and much more about the Pokémon universe. We love exploring every corner of the Pokémon world and sharing our passion with you.

Our values

Passion for Pokémon

The passion we feel for Pokémon drives us to excel and the positive impact our brand has on our colleagues, fans, players and communities.

Dedication to quality

We are committed to maintaining and encouraging the highest standards, always looking for new ways to learn and improve, while encouraging and supporting everyone else to do the same.

Defy expectations

We demonstrate curiosity and creativity, and use these qualities to develop innovative ideas, solve complex problems, and identify improvements.

Cultivate relationships

Our motto is "team first", so we create and strengthen collaborative relationships with all our colleagues, both internal and external, to achieve our goals and bring joy to our community.

Integrity and respect

We treat each person with respect and empathy to create an open and welcoming environment.

Delight consumers

We listen to our customers and stakeholders, and do everything in our power to deliver a unique Pokémon experience.
