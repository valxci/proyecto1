Favorites Pokemon:

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Plant/Venom-type Pokémon. From birth, it possesses a seed on its back that gradually grows along with it.

Charizard

Charizard is a Fire/Flying-type Pokémon. Its breath is capable of melting rocks and causing forest fires by flapping its wings.

Gyarados

Gyarados is a Water/Flying type Pokémon. It is known for its fierce and destructive temperament, and can wreak great havoc when enraged.
